Topics

This event will highlight cutting-edge tools, technologies, and approaches to dissect the tremendous complexity of the human immune system:

  • Highly Multiplexed Single-Cell Phenotyping
  • Translational Single-Cell RNA Sequencing
  • Protein Barcoded Single Cell CRISPR Screening
  • CITE-Seq & Cell Hashing Advancements
  • Single Cell V(D)J Immune Profiling
  • Multiplexed Immunohistochemistry
  • Multiplexed Ion Beam Imaging (MIBI)
  • Integrative Data Analysis Data Management
